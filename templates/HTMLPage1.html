EMIT
· Sensors are becoming increasingly popular in electronics and DIY projects. Various sensors are available, from temperature sensors to motion sensors, which can be integrated into projects to improve functionality and automation. One of these sensors, which has recently gained huge popularity, is a laser emitter. sensor.

· The magic of laser sensors lies in their precision and accuracy, which makes them ideal for a wide range of applications. They emit a beam of laser light and detect reflected light, making them suitable for distance measurement, obstacle detection and even for security applications.

Dust suspended in the air can heat or cool the atmosphere and the Earth's surface. This heating or cooling effect is the focus of NASA's mission to investigate the sources of mineral dust on the Earth's surface (EMIT)

Dust can contribute to the formation of clouds or changes in the chemical composition of the atmosphere.

When exposed to snow or ice, mineral dust can increase the absorption of sunlight and accelerate melting

Mineral dust in the air can impair visibility or harm human health.
